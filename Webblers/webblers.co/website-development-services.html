Our Process How a Red Stone Website is Made

We follow a simple, five-step process for our web development services to help streamline your project and add some creative energy to it!

Requirements

First, we understand the requirements and what you need for the project, as well as gathering data for future research and analysis.

Analysis

Here, we analyze your requirements, compare it with market trends and our past projects, and determine how to proceed.

Prototyping

We create website wireframes, content strategy decks, logo concepts, and everything else needed to give you an idea of the final product.

Development

Here we conduct the development and testing alongside it. This is the lengthiest part of the project, but it’s worth it!

Deployment

We create the final product and prepare any marketing or social media content required for taking your website live to an eagerly awaiting audience!
